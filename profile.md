## Professional Summary

I'm a software engineer with experience designing and supporting backend systems within regulated and operationally complex environments, particularly across Banking-as-a-Service and FinCrime domains.

My work has focused on scalable API development, workflow improvement, and understanding how technical decisions impact both operational teams and end users. I'm especially interested in the space between technical systems and real-world usage — simplifying complexity, improving maintainability, and designing solutions that genuinely support the people using them day to day.

I enjoy environments where curiosity, collaboration, and thoughtful engineering are valued. Much of my experience has involved navigating ambiguity, balancing overlapping requirements, and translating operational needs into practical technical approaches.

Alongside my technical career, I've spent recent years travelling throughout New Zealand and working in people-focused roles outside the tech industry. These experiences strengthened my adaptability, communication style, and ability to work effectively with a wide range of people, while reinforcing my interest in human-centred problem solving.

---

## Strengths at a Glance

- **Backend & API Development**: Experience designing and supporting backend services, onboarding APIs, and modular system components within large-scale applications.

- **Systems & Workflow Thinking**: Strong focus on understanding how technical systems interact with operational processes, identifying friction points, and improving maintainability and usability over time.

- **Translating Complexity**: Comfortable working across technical and non-technical perspectives to clarify requirements, surface hidden constraints, and turn ambiguous problems into structured solutions.

- **Operational Engineering**: Experience supporting production systems through incident response, monitoring, release processes, and investigation of live operational issues.

- **Adaptability & Curiosity**: Quick to learn unfamiliar technologies, domains, and workflows, with a strong curiosity for understanding how systems function beneath the surface.
