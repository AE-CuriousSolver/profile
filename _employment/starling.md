**Cross-Domain & External Collaboration**

Worked with external banking partners, product teams, compliance, fraud investigators, and engineering teams to ensure system changes aligned with both operational reality and regulatory constraints.
Developed a strong understanding of how external client expectations and internal operational workflows interact, particularly in onboarding and fraud-related systems.

**Service & Operational Support**

Contributed across the wider platform through incident response, production support, release processes, and system monitoring.
Strengthened engineering practices through participation in operational workflows, improving visibility of system behaviour and supporting shared understanding across teams.
